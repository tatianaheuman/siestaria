Siestaria is an ongoing artistic research project by Tatiana Heuman and Florencia Curci. It considers radical rest and listening in dreams as forms of resistance, and the oneiric realm as a refuge for the parts of ourselves that refuse to adjust to the logics of efficiency and optimization.

The project takes many forms — workshops, installations, dream concerts — built around dream listening, dream translation and oneiric cartographies. Rather than treating rest as a pause or an absence, Siestaria proposes repose as a generative ground for reimagining how we live, relate and resist together.

Tatiana and Florencia met as drummers in the experimental music underground of Buenos Aires, and have collaborated across constellations such as the transfeminist sound platform #VIVAS. Siestaria has been presented at HKW Berlin, CASo Buenos Aires, and Festival Tsonami, Valparaíso, among others.
